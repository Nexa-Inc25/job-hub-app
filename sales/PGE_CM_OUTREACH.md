*"Thanks for taking the time. I'll show you exactly how contractors can use this to submit better as-builts faster. Feel free to interrupt with questions."*

### Foreman Workflow (5 min)
1. Show mobile app on phone
2. Open a sample job (use a PM number they'd recognize)
3. Show how foreman captures a unit:
   - Select pole type (T1-T5)
   - Enter quantity
   - Take photo with GPS
   - Add notes
4. Emphasize: **"Works offline in remote areas"**

1. Show document structure:
   - Pole Bill
   - EC Tag

   - Equipment & Pole Info

   - Map
   - CCSC
