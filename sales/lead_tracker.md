## Target Company Research List

### California PG&E Contractors
Research these on LinkedIn and company websites:

1. **Alvah Contractors** - Pole replacement, CWA contractor
2. **Par Electrical Contractors** - T&D construction
3. **Henkels & McCoy** - Major utility contractor
4. **MYR Group** - Electrical construction
5. **Michels Corporation** - Underground/overhead
6. **Sturgeon Electric** - Western region
7. **Intren** - Distribution construction
8. **Asplundh** - Vegetation + some construction
9. **Pike Electric** - T&D services
10. **Quanta Services** (subs) - Various operating companies

### Where to Find Contacts
- **LinkedIn**: Search "[Company] + Foreman + PG&E"
- **LinkedIn**: Search "[Company] + Operations Manager"
- **Company websites**: Look for leadership/contact pages
- **Bid lists**: CPUC contractor lists, utility RFP responses

### Trade Associations (for networking)
- **CUEA** - California Utilities Emergency Association
- **NECA** - National Electrical Contractors Association
- **NUCA** - National Utility Contractors Association
- **DistribuTECH** - Utility conference
