## Current Challenges (Based on Discovery)

- [ ] Foremen spend 30-45 minutes per job on paper unit tickets
- [ ] As-built packages assembled manually, prone to missing documents
- [ ] Billing delayed waiting for paper to come in from the field
- [ ] Claims rejected due to incomplete or incorrect documentation
- [ ] No real-time visibility into job progress

## Proposed Solution

### FieldLedger Platform

| Capability | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| **Mobile Unit Capture** | Foremen log units, quantities, and photos on their phones |
| **Offline Mode** | Works in remote areas, syncs when connected |
| **Digital As-Builts** | Auto-assembles documents in correct order |
| **Approval Workflow** | Supervisors review and approve remotely |
| **Oracle Export** | FBDI format for direct AP submission |
| **Analytics Dashboard** | Real-time job status and billing metrics |
