## Foreman Quick Start Guide

### Installing the App (2 minutes)

1. Open **Chrome** on your phone
2. Go to: **www.fieldledger.io**
3. Log in with your email and password
4. Tap the **"Add to Home Screen"** prompt (or use Chrome menu > Add to Home Screen)
5. You now have FieldLedger as an app icon!

### Daily Workflow

#### Morning: Start Your Day
1. Open FieldLedger
2. Check **"My Jobs"** for today's assignments
3. Review job details and crew instructions

#### On Site: Capture Units
1. Open the job
2. Tap **"Add Unit"**
3. Select item from price book (e.g., "POLE-T3-ACC")
4. Enter quantity
5. **Take photo** of completed work
6. GPS location captured automatically
7. Tap **"Save"**

#### End of Day: Submit
1. Review your unit entries
2. Tap **"Submit for Review"**
3. GF will be notified to approve

### Offline Mode
- FieldLedger works **without internet**!
- Capture units while in dead zones
- Data syncs automatically when you get signal
- Blue badge shows pending sync items

---

## GF/PM Approval Workflow

### Reviewing Units

1. Log in to FieldLedger (desktop or mobile)
2. Go to **Billing > Unit Approval**
3. Review pending unit entries
4. Check photos and GPS data
5. **Approve** or **Reject** with notes

### Generating Claims

1. Go to **Billing > Claims**
2. Click **"Create New Claim"**
3. Select approved units to include
4. Review totals
5. Submit for PM approval

### Exporting to Oracle

1. Go to **Billing > Claims**
2. Select approved claim(s)
3. Click **"Export FBDI"**
4. Download CSV file
5. Upload to Oracle Fusion Cloud

## Common Questions

### "What if I make a mistake?"
- Units can be edited before approval
- GF/PM can reject and request corrections
- Nothing is final until claim is exported

### "What about our existing paper process?"
- During pilot, you can run both in parallel
- Foremen capture in FieldLedger, paper as backup
- After pilot, FieldLedger becomes primary

### "Is our data secure?"
- All data encrypted in transit and at rest
- Multi-factor authentication available
- Your data is isolated from other companies
- SOC 2 compliance in progress

### "What happens after the pilot?"
- We review metrics together
- If successful, transition to paid subscription
- Pricing: $49-79/user/month (volume discounts available)
- No long-term contract required
