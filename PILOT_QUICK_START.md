## üìã Core Workflow

### 1. Create a Work Order
- Click **+ New Work Order**
- Fill in PM Number, customer, job address
- Upload any existing PDFs (AI will extract data automatically)

### 2. Pre-Field Preparation
- Open the job from the dashboard
- Complete the pre-field checklist
- Upload site maps, permits, existing documents

### 3. Field Work
- Crew members can view job details on mobile
- Upload photos directly to job folders
- Mark tasks complete as work progresses

### 4. Document Management
- All documents organized in folders by type
- Double-click any PDF to view/annotate
- Documents can be approved/rejected by supervisors

### 5. Job Completion
- Update job status as work progresses
- Export data for billing via CSV export button

---

## üìÅ File System

Each job has pre-configured folders:
- **Agreements** - Contracts, NDAs
- **Bid Sheets** - Estimates, pricing
- **Crew Sheets** - Daily logs, time tracking
- **Site Surveys** - Photos, assessments
- **Drawings** - Maps, schematics
- **Photos** - Field documentation

### Uploading Documents
1. Navigate to the job
2. Select the appropriate folder
3. Click **Upload** or drag & drop files

### Viewing/Editing PDFs
- Double-click any PDF to open the editor
- Add text, checkmarks, or signatures
- Click **Save** to store your changes

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Why isn't my job showing up?**  
A: Check that you're assigned to the job. If you're a crew member, ask your foreman.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**  
A: Contact your company admin.

**Q: Can I delete a job?**  
A: Only admins can delete jobs. Regular users can archive jobs.

**Q: Where do uploaded photos go?**  
A: They're saved in the "Photos" folder for that job automatically.
